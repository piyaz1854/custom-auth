Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, share or sell any personal data to third parties.

All user data (email, name, roles, permissions) is stored securely on our local server and is used solely to demonstrate role-based access control features. No data is used for commercial purposes.

Passwords are stored in a hashed format using bcrypt. We do not log raw passwords or tokens.

By using this system, you agree that all actions are for demonstration or testing purposes only, and no real sensitive information is required or expected.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, feel free to contact me.
